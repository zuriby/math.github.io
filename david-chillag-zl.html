The Chillag Award Context

The Technion grants the "Chillag Award" for excellence in teaching. There is a profound connection between his research and his pedagogy.

Just as his research on Table Algebras sought to reveal the hidden, unifying structures beneath complex groups, his teaching of Linear Algebra revealed the internal structure of vector spaces to students. He did not just teach formulas; he taught the "character" of mathematics.

Legacy in Practice

  • ✦ Recipient of the Yanai Prize for Excellence in Academic Education.

  • ✦ Research method: Look for the "Zeros" (missing info) to find the structure.
